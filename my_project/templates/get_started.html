Help & Help

Neighbor, Communitys and Organizations

Read about it!

The Idea of the website is to enable communitys to connect in order to help and share among the community members.
This website intends to provide a platform where one can offer or request help / goods / guidance.
One can create a post with their needs and offers so that others are able to see the post and respond to it.

In the coming version of this website a matching according to the location will be offered for local communitys.

Get started!

To get started navigate to "register" and register your account, sing in.
Now you can go to your profile page and enter additional information about yourself.
This is recommended before creating a post.
After that you can follow the instructions below on "how to use it!".

How to use it!

On the "Browse" section you can browse all posts. Your own post will be highlighted for you.
On the post you will find information regarding the request/offer and you can click on the button "more information" to excess additinal infos like "Location".
On "Create" you can create your own post. Fill in the fields with your post information and submit it. (right now you are not able to change these later)
